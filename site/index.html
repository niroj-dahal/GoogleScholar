About

Dr. Niroj Dahal, Ph.D. (ORCID: 0000-0001-7646-1186) is a lecturer at the Department of STEAM Education, Kathmandu University School of Education, Nepal. His research spans ICT in education, artificial intelligence (AI) and GenAI in pedagogy, qualitative and alternative methodologies, mathematics education, open and distance e‑learning, and transformative research. He has taught for over two decades, presented at numerous national and international venues, and serves as Managing Editor of the Journal of Transformative Praxis (JrTP) and as an editorial board member of TQR.
